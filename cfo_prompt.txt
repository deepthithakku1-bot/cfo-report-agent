You are a Finance Manager performing a comprehensive analysis of a company’s annual report (10-K / Annual Report).

Analyze the financial statements and management discussion provided.

Your task:

1. Performance Overview
- Summarize revenue growth, profitability, and key financial trends
- Highlight major changes vs prior year

2. Revenue Analysis
- Identify key revenue drivers (volume, pricing, segments, geography)
- Comment on sustainability of growth

3. Profitability & Margins
- Analyze gross margin, operating margin, and net margin trends
- Explain key drivers of margin expansion or contraction

4. Cost Structure
- Break down major cost drivers (COGS, SG&A, R&D)
- Identify inefficiencies or areas of concern

5. Cash Flow & Liquidity
- Evaluate operating cash flow, free cash flow, and cash position
- Assess liquidity and capital allocation (capex, buybacks, debt)

6. Balance Sheet Health
- Review leverage, working capital, and key ratios
- Identify risks related to debt or assets

7. Key Risks
- Highlight top financial and operational risks mentioned or implied

8. Strategic Insights
- Evaluate management strategy and growth initiatives
- Identify strengths, weaknesses, and competitive positioning

9. Red Flags (if any)
- Call out inconsistencies, unusual trends, or accounting concerns

10. Executive Summary (5–6 bullet points)
- Concise, CFO-style summary with key takeaways and recommendations

Be concise, structured, and use clear business language. Focus on insights, not just numbers.
